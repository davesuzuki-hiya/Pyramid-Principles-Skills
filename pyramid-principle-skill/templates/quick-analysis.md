# Quick Analysis Example

## SCQA Frame

**Situation:** PLG trial signups have increased 40% MoM after the new landing page launch.

**Complication:** However, trial-to-paid conversion has dropped from 1.2% to 0.9% in the same period, suggesting we're attracting lower-intent users.

**Question:** Should we revert the landing page changes to restore conversion quality?

**Hypothesis/Answer:** No - we should optimize the onboarding flow rather than revert, as total paid conversions (volume × rate) are still up 5%.

---

## Quick Issue Breakdown

1. **Traffic Quality:**
   - Evidence: New traffic has 60% lower activation rate (Day 1 feature usage)

2. **Absolute Conversions:**
   - Evidence: Total paid users up 5% despite lower rate (volume compensates)

3. **Cohort Trajectory:**
   - Evidence: Need 30-day data to confirm - too early for final judgment

---

## So What?

- From the evidence above, the key insight is: Volume growth is masking a quality problem that may compound over time.
- This matters because: If we don't address activation, we'll scale a leaky funnel.

---

## Recommendation

**Recommended Action:** Keep the new landing page but implement activation-focused onboarding A/B test within 2 weeks.

**Confidence Level:** [X] Medium (some gaps)

**Key Risk/Caveat:** If Day-30 conversion cohort data shows <0.7% conversion, we should reconsider.

---

## Next Step

**Immediate Action:** Product team to spec activation experiment by Friday. Schedule cohort review for March 15.
